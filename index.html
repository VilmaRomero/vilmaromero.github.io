Vilma Romero

Lecturer of Statistics

University of Lima

I am a Lecturer in the Faculty of Engineering at the University of Lima located in Peru. My research interests include Data Visualization, Statistical Learning and Data Science.

Also, I am part of the R-Ladies Global community and the chapter R-Ladies Lima.
